Why CUtrust

Partnership Advantages

Recognized Credit Union Expertise

For several decades, we've been recognized as experts serving the needs of credit unions of all sizes. Our highly qualified in-house staff focuses exclusively on credit union pension and benefits plans.

Up-to-Date Advice

Our staff regularly attends conferences, industry committees, and roundtable discussions to stay on top of complex pension and benefits issues facing today's credit unions.

Economies of Scale

By bringing credit unions together, our economies of scale enable us to allocate more dollars to claims and claims reduction strategies, and less on external fees and overhead costs.

Large Industry Presence

Our large size and presence in the industry allow us to exercise more influence with service providers, resulting in beneficial underwriting arrangements and stronger negotiating power.

Enhanced Coverage

Our bulk purchasing power allows us to offer better levels of coverage at cost-effective rates, meaning you can affordably offer your employees more and better coverage options.

Customized Benefits & Coverage

Our programs are adaptable to your needs — choose what type and level of benefits to provide, who is eligible, and the level of cost sharing you want to apply.

Flexibility

Choose from our traditional benefits program, a defined benefit retirement plan, a defined contribution arrangement, or a flexible benefits program where employees create a customized plan.

Fair Pricing

We're not in the business of profiting from clients who effectively own the CU Trust. Costs are based on your credit union's claim experience — not on premium cross-subsidization.
