About CUtrust

Credit Union Pension & Benefits Trust is an umbrella entity comprising two core programs:

1. BC Credit Union Employees' Pension Plan

2. BC Credit Union Employee Benefits Trust

Together, we support participating credit unions in British Columbia by governing and administering multi-employer pension and benefits programs for employees and beneficiaries. We operate in a complex, multi-employer environment, designed to help employers deliver valuable programs efficiently while serving members' long-term financial well-being.
